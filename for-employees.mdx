# What is Branco?

Branco is a performance and feedback platform designed to help teams stay aligned, focused, and continuously improving. It brings together feedback, goals, and career development into a single, structured experience.

**Branco helps by:**

- **Collecting feedback automatically through Slack**, making it easy to share and receive input in the flow of work

- **Aligning the company around its vision, strategy, and goals**, so everyone understands what matters most

- **Providing structure for 1:1s** between employees and managers

- **Making career paths and progress visible**, so growth expectations are clear

# How Branco Helps You

Branco is designed to make your work life clearer, more structured, and more transparent.

**Using Branco helps you:**

- **Share feedback easily** with teammates and managers directly from Slack

- **Show how your work connects to company goals**, so your impact is visible

- **Have more meaningful 1:1s**, with clear talking points and follow-ups

- **Understand your career path and expectations**, including what growth looks like over time
